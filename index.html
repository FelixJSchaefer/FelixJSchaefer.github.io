Game Summary

Cozy Tales is a cozy open world exploration game. The player traverses a beautifully stylized landscape filled with hidden landmarks and scenic beauty.

Important landmarks can be marked to create maps for adventurers. Also, unique items and artifacts throughout the world can be collected.

The game has a strong focus on relaxation and enjoyment, with no combat or high-stress situations.

Role & Responsibilities

Roadmap & Milestones Planning

  • Outlined key objectives and segmented them into attainable milestones by crafting a comprehensive Roadmap.
  • Engaged in in-depth discussions with the development team to establish a realistic timeline, ensuring resource allocation and flexibility for project adjustments.
  • Assessed the development pipeline for potential bottlenecks and dependencies to maintain smooth progression.
  • Guided decision-making through meticulous risk assessments and leveraging an understanding of team capabilities, ensuring the project stayed on a realistic and successful path.

Agile Workflow Management

  • Maintained oversight of the product backlog and monitored sprint progress, ensuring a cohesive development process.
  • Facilitated the adoption of Codecks for project tracking, providing comprehensive training and documentation to streamline team engagement with the tool.
  • Established the framework for project structure and team dynamics, each team member with clear roles and accountability.
  • Integrated an Agile methodology tailored to our team's unique qualities and requirements, specifically employing Scrum principles with a designated Product Owner.

Game Design & Engine Work

  • Documentation: Systematically documented game design features, detailing task specifications and completion criteria.
  • Narrative Design: Collaborated with the Game Design team to enhance narrative development, formulating engaging quests and writing dialogue and backstory elements.
  • Programming: Developed and programmed interactive features and user interfaces, including an AI letter-writing system, menus (main, options, pause), as well as an end-screen with trigger functionality.
  • Content Integration: Executed the integration of diverse content into Unreal Engine, ranging from 3D models and quest elements to dialogue text and voice-over files.
